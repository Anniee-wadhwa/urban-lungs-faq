Urban Lungs – Frequently Asked Questions

What is Urban Lungs paint?

Urban Lungs is a sustainable, eco-conscious paint infused with Titanium Dioxide (TiO₂) that helps clean the air while providing aesthetic and thermal benefits.

How does Urban Lungs reduce pollution?

It uses photocatalytic technology that reacts with sunlight to break down harmful pollutants such as NOx and VOCs, turning painted surfaces into air-purifying panels.

Can it be used on outdoor surfaces or hoardings?

Yes, it's perfect for outdoor use, including hoardings, where sunlight maximizes its pollution-neutralizing effects.

Is Urban Lungs paint fire-resistant?

Yes, it's water-based and non-flammable, significantly safer than traditional solvent-based alternatives.

What is the cost per litre?

The paint is priced between ₹170–₹180 per litre, making it an affordable alternative with added environmental benefits.

Is it safe for residential use?

Yes, it's free from VOCs and toxins, suitable for homes, schools, hospitals, and any space where health and safety matter.

Can it help reduce indoor temperature?

Yes, the reflective white base helps reduce indoor temperatures by 4–5°C, contributing to energy savings and comfort.

Is the paint durable and washable?

Definitely. Urban Lungs is engineered for a long-lasting, easy-to-maintain surface finish.

Can it be tinted or is it only available in white?

While white is optimal for reflective benefits, it can be tinted using safe pigments to suit varied interior or exterior aesthetics.

Does it require special application techniques?

No, Urban Lungs is compatible with standard painting tools like brushes, rollers, and sprays.
